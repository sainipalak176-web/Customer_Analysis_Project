# 📊 Customer Purchase Analysis Project

A complete data analysis project on 1,00,000 customer records
using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

---

## 🗂️ Dataset
- **File:** customer_data_100k.csv
- **Records:** 1,00,000
- **Columns:** Name, Age, City, Purchase_Amount
- **Cities:** Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

- Data Loading & Exploration
- Null Value & Duplicate Check (529 duplicates removed)
- Basic Statistical Analysis
- City-wise Total & Average Purchase
- Age Group Analysis
- Top 5 Customers by Purchase

---

## 📈 Visualizations Created

- Bar Chart – City Wise Total Purchase
- Histogram – Age Distribution
- Box Plot – Purchase Amount by City
- Bar Chart – Average Purchase by City
- Bar Chart – Average Purchase by Age Group
- Pie Chart – Customers by City
- Scatter Plot – Age vs Purchase Amount
- Heatmap – Correlation Matrix
- Dashboard – All charts combined

---

## 🏆 Key Findings

- 💰 Highest buying city: **Mumbai**
- 👥 Total clean records: **99,471**
- 📊 Average purchase: ~**Rs. 10,046**
- 🏙️ All 3 cities have almost equal customer share (~33%)
